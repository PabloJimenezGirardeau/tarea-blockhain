► Anomaly Detection Model
Exponential distribution · MLE parameter estimation
Bitcoin block arrival is a memoryless Poisson process — each hash attempt is independent. Inter-block times follow an Exp(λ) distribution. λ is estimated via MLE: λ̂ = 1/x̄ rather than assuming 1/600. Blocks outside the central 90% are flagged as anomalous.
